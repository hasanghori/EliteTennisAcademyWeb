Coaches

Naeem Saleem

Naeem began playing tennis at the age of 6. He started competing in USTA tournaments when he was 9 years old and began winning Midwest level events and gained national recognition shortly after. He has consistently been ranked among the top 10 players in Michigan and has been awarded numerous accolades from the USTA. In high school, Naeem was selected to play the #1 singles position for his varsity team for four straight years and led his team to the State Finals three times out of four. He was a 2-time All-State Honorable Mention awardee and received All-State (among the most prestigious awards given by the MHSAA) his senior year.

Vishwaas Nayak

Vishwaas has been a varsity player ever since his freshman year of high school. He was an integral part of the team and was responsible for leading them to three State Finals appearances. He has consistently had a winning record throughout high school and was responsible for being the first person in Canton High School to earn a point at the State Championships. In his senior year, he finished the season with an impressive 18-2 record and as the captain, led his team to States for the third time.

David Wu

David is currently the #1 singles player at Plymouth High School. He is a rising senior and has earned the right to play at this esteemed position ever since he entered high school. David participates in many USTA tournaments and his spectacular league record has made him a top 20 player in Michigan. He is among the highest rated players in his class and also holds one of the most flawless high school match records in the state. He is looking to lead his team to the State Finals for the second consecutive year as a Plymouth Wildcat.
