Our Coaches

  • Naeem Saleem

    Naeem has been playing tennis ever since the age of six. He was fortunate enough to have international exposure to the game and learnt most of his tennis as he grew up in India. When he returned to the U.S. at the age of twelve, he started competing in USTA tournaments and began winning Midwest level events and gained national recognition. He has consistently ranked among the top 10 players in Michigan and has been awarded numerous accolades from the USTA. In high school, Naeem was selected to play the #1 singles position for his varsity team for four straight years and led his team to the state finals three times out of four. He was a 2-time All-State Honorable Mention awardee and received All-State (among the most prestige award given by the MHSAA) his senior year.

  • Vishwaas Nayak

    Vishwaas has been a varsity player ever since freshmen year. He was an integral part of the team and was responsible for leading them to three State Finals. He has consistently had a winning record throughout high school and was responsible for being the first person in Canton High School to earn a point at the State Championships His senior year, he finished the season with an impressive 18-2 record and as a the captain, led his team to States for the third time.

  • David Wu

    David is the current #1 singles player at Plymouth High School. He is currenty a sophomore and has earned the right to play this position for the first two years of his high school tennis career. David participates in many USTA tournaments and his spectacular league record has made him a top 20 player in the state. He is among the highest rated players in his class and also holds one of the most flawless high school match record in the state. He is looking to lead his team to the State Finals for his last two years as a Plymouth Wildcat.
