Our Mission

With a lack of tennis clubs/camps in the Plymouth-Canton community, our goal is to present people of all ages with an opportunity to learn the game, have fun, and meet new people along the way!

Our Coaches

Naeem Saleem

Naeem began playing tennis at age 6 and started competing in USTA tournaments by 9, quickly earning national recognition. He has consistently ranked among Michigan’s top 10 players and earned multiple USTA accolades. In high school, he played #1 singles all four years, leading his varsity team to three State Finals appearances. He was named All-State Honorable Mention twice and earned full All-State honors during his senior year. After recently graduating from the University of Michigan, Naeem continues to channel his passion for tennis into coaching the next generation. As a USTA-certified coach with over 6 years of coaching experience, Naeem has trained players of all ages and skill levels, from young beginners to elite varsity athletes and Division 1 college competitors. His students have achieved notable success in USTA tournaments and high school tennis programs across Michigan. Naeem is known not just for his technical expertise, but for his ability to build confidence, discipline, and a deep love for the game in each of his students. Many families across Michigan have come to trust his coaching approach, which combines strategic instruction with a genuine investment in each player’s growth.

Vishwaas Nayak

Vishwaas has been a varsity player since his freshman year and played a key role in leading Canton High School to three State Finals appearances. Known for his consistency, he maintained a strong winning record throughout high school and made history as the first player from Canton to earn a win at the State Championships. With over 6 years of coaching experience and USTA certification, Vishwaas brings both competitive insight, as well as a patient and encouraging style to every session. He has worked with players of all ages, from beginners to experienced high school athletes, helping them build solid fundamentals and mental resilience. Known for his calm demeanor and kindness, Vishwaas creates a welcoming environment that helps players feel supported, motivated, and eager to improve. His athletes have grown into confident and well-rounded players who thrive in both competitive school teams and regional USTA tournaments.

Aadi Huria

Aadi made his mark at Salem High School as a top singles player, consistently posting a stellar record throughout his varsity career. As team captain, he helped lead his team to the State Championships, earning a reputation for his strategic mindset and composed on-court presence. His deep love for the sport extends beyond competition. He is also the founder and director of Serve to Serve Academy (s2sa.net), a nonprofit organization dedicated to making tennis accessible to underprivileged youth. Through this initiative, Aadi shares the game he loves with new communities and inspires the next generation of players. With 3 years of coaching experience, Aadi specializes in working with elementary, middle, and high school athletes, guiding them from foundational skills to confident and match-ready players. His success in developing young players stems from his attention to detail, positive attitude, and ability to make his lessons both fun and effective.

Benjamin Fernandez

Benjamin was ranked as the #1 singles player at Plymouth High School for three consecutive years, establishing himself as a dominant force in Michigan high school tennis. He reached a career-high junior ranking of #9 in the state and was widely recognized for his speed, discipline, and competitive drive. In addition to his on-court success, Benjamin brings a strong background in fitness and conditioning, helping players improve their strength, agility, and endurance to compete at their highest level.

Avneet Singh

Avneet led his high school team to the State Championships as team captain and #1 singles player during his senior year. Known for his strong leadership and reliable game, he quickly built a reputation as a dedicated and passionate player. Today, Avneet channels that same energy into teaching young athletes the fundamentals of tennis. With a warm and approachable style, he connects easily with kids and makes learning fun, helping them develop confidence, coordination, and a love for the game.

Shyrod Sajadpour

Shyrod was a standout player at Salem High School, where he led his team to multiple deep playoff runs and finished as a State Finalist during his high school career. Known for his powerful game and mental toughness, Shyrod has long been recognized as one of the top competitors in the region. He now uses that high-level experience to guide advanced players through the mental and technical demands of tournament play, with a focus on precision, match strategy, and consistent improvement.
