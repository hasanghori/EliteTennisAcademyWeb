Our Mission

With a lack of tennis clubs/camps in the Plymouth-Canton community, our goal is to present people of all ages with an opportunity to learn the game, have fun, and meet new people along the way!

Our Coaches

Naeem Saleem

Naeem began playing tennis at age 6 and started competing in USTA tournaments by 9, quickly earning national recognition. He has consistently ranked among Michigan’s top 10 players and earned multiple USTA accolades. In high school, he played #1 singles all four years, leading his varsity team to three State Finals appearances. He was named All-State Honorable Mention twice and earned full All-State honors his senior year.

Vishwaas Nayak

Vishwaas has been a varsity player since his freshman year and played a key role in leading Canton High School to three State Finals appearances. Known for his consistency, he maintained a strong winning record throughout high school and made history as the first player from Canton to earn a point at the State Championships. As a senior and team captain, he finished with an impressive 18-2 record.

David Wu

David recently completed his final year of high school, playing #1 singles for three consecutive seasons. A regular USTA competitor, his standout league record earned him a top-20 ranking in Michigan. In his senior year, he reached the State Finals semifinals at #2 singles—becoming the first player from Plymouth High School to do so—and earned All-State Honorable Mention honors from the MHSAA.
